About Me

I am passionate about creating innovative solutions that empower people to do more, know more, and be more.

With 15 years of experience designing robust platforms across dynamic sectors—including AdTech, EdTech, Telecom, Customer Loyalty, Pharma, Logistics, and Finance — I excel at transforming complex challenges into user-centric, scalable products.

My expertise lies in bridging technology and business needs to deliver impactful results that drive growth and enable success for both organizations and their customers.

Why do I design

Because to design is humane. Design reaches people and improves their life. Therefore, I design.

Who Am I

I am a work in progress. A lifelong learner, I like to explore possibilities and solutions. I believe that the meaningful application of design and business strategy can have tremendous potential to impact lives.

How I design

  • I listen. I understand people, their emotions, cognition, and inner drive.
  • I believe in the simplicity of the progressive iterations. I prioritize usability and inclusiveness over the aesthetics. Design is for all and so is the web.
  • I prefer to ship products early and keep on improving them based on the feedback cycles.
  • I value teamwork. Design is all about collaboration, second opinions, and feedback.

What I bring

  • I have experience working across the complete UX design cycle from user research to usability testing.
  • I have an eye for the products. I have launched solutions, nurtured them to maturity, maintained them, and have later merged them into other product offerings.
  • I understand data. I know how to draw insights from it.
  • I understand technology. I understand frontend coding, SEO, digital marketing, web accessibility, agile, and scrums.

Whom do I work for

I am the advocate of my users.

Whom do I work with

Everyone in the ecosystem! My cross-functional experience helps me to easily collaborate with fellow designers, business analysts, scrum masters, product owners, developers, QAs, translators, and others. I can understand their perspective and terminologies, and therefore can communicate effectively.
