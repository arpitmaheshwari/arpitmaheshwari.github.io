§ 02

Manifesto

Intelligent systems fail when users don't understand them.
Design distrust first, trust second.

01

Design the failure state first.

AI is wrong often. The happy path is 30% of the work. I design what happens when the model is uncertain, when data is missing, or when confidence is low.

02

Show the why.

Users don't trust black boxes. Explainability—showing data provenance, confidence levels, and model weights—isn't a nice-to-have. It's the product.

03

Accessible by default.

An AI dashboard that only works for sighted mouse users fails half the audience. I build WCAG AA compliance into the first sprint, not the last.
