## Messaging Principles

**Do:**
- Reference something specific about their product ("I saw you're building X — I've solved that exact design problem in Y")
- Link to one artifact (portfolio case, Pattern #1, or a quick Loom)
- Ask a real question, not a pitch ("How are non-technical stakeholders using your AI features?")
- Keep it to 50–80 words max

**Don't:**
- Generic "I'd love to chat" (spam signal)
- Long pitch about your services
- Multiple links or CTAs (confuses decision)
- LinkedIn connection requests before messaging (in DM, not connection first)

**Tone:** Peer-to-peer, curious, specific. Like you're asking them a real question about their design challenges.
