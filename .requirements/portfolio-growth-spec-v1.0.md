## Business Context

### Primary Objectives
1. **Generate high-value freelance/contract projects** — Direct revenue from client engagements
2. **Attract full-time employment opportunities** — Signal to hiring managers across organizations
3. **Build personal brand & thought leadership** — Establish authority in AI/design space

### Target Audience
- **Broad audience** — No single segment prioritized equally  
  - Enterprise decision-makers (Fortune 500 / large tech)
  - Founders & hiring managers (startups / growth-stage)
  - Direct clients seeking freelancers/consultants

### Current Challenge
**Not generating enough inbound leads** — Portfolio exists but isn't driving sufficient business opportunities

---

## Current State Assessment

### Strengths (from portfolio)
- Well-executed, accessible design  
- 15 years of relevant industry experience  
- Clear positioning in AI & data-intensive products  
- Presence across multiple high-value sectors (AdTech, FinTech, EdTech, Telecom)  
- Demonstrates design maturity & systems thinking  

### Known Gaps (root cause: discoverability)
- **Low organic traffic** — Few leads arrive at portfolio in first place (vs. conversion problem)
- **Limited presence in buyer search journeys** — Not visible when targets are actively looking
- **Unclear positioning for search** — Hard to find via relevant keywords (e.g., "AI product designer," "fintech UX," etc.)
- **No content strategy** — Portfolio is static destination, not a discovery/education funnel
- **Traffic source unknown** — Unclear which channels *should* drive leads (SEO, referral, social, direct)

### Secondary gaps (to address after visibility)
- **No measurable proof of impact** — Case studies lack business outcomes (revenue, users, retention)
- **Missing conversion path** — Unclear what action visitors should take after viewing
