### Questions to Ask While Testing

1. **At 32px (Substack icon):** Can you identify what it is?
2. **At 128px (hero/profile):** Does it feel "Creative Clarity"?
3. **On dark background:** Does it work with white background?
4. **Memorable:** Could you describe it to someone?
5. **Longevity:** Does it feel timeless or trendy?
