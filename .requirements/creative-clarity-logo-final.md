## Design Rationale

**Why Light Burst works for Creative Clarity:**

✅ **Radiating rays** = Inspiration breaking through, creative spark  
✅ **Staggered opacity** = Energy, movement, depth  
✅ **Clarity circles** = Resolution, focus, clarity  
✅ **Centered dot** = Pure essence, clarity achieved  
✅ **Gold color** = Your brand, warmth, premium feel  
✅ **Simple geometry** = Works at all sizes, timeless  

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Do ✅
- Use on light backgrounds (white, #F2EDE4, light grays)
- Use white/cream version on dark backgrounds
- Scale proportionally
- Keep minimum 32px for readability
- Pair with "Creative Clarity" text when possible

### Don't ❌
- Rotate or skew the logo
- Change colors unless using alt palette
- Add shadows or effects
- Crowd with other graphics
- Use below 24px (becomes unclear)
