# Portfolio Growth Strategy — Summary
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-21

---

## The Challenge
**Few leads arriving at portfolio** → Discoverability problem, not conversion problem.

---

## The Solution
**Google Search visibility strategy** — Become discoverable when target buyers search for designer expertise.

---

## Key Strategic Decisions (LOCKED)

### 1. Primary Channel: Google Search
- **Why:** High-intent buyers actively searching (freelance projects + employment leads + thought leadership)
- **Timeline:** Compound growth over 12 months (not quick win)
- **Investment:** Content + case study optimization + backlinks

### 2. Case Study Approach: NDA-Conscious
- **Problem:** Real metrics are NDA-bound
- **Solution:** Use alternative credibility signals
  - Problem context instead of outcomes
  - Testimonials instead of metrics
  - Client logos + industry examples
  - Years in space + range of sectors
- **Deliverable:** Text-optimized case studies (searchable, keyword-targeted)

### 3. Content Vehicle: Substack (Not On-Site Blog)
- **Why:** Substack has better discoverability + SEO potential
- **Cadence:** 1 article/month (12/year)
- **Focus:** Broad "AI & data-intensive design" (not niche-specific)
- **Distribution:** Substack + LinkedIn + 2-3 guest posts/year on authority sites
- **Audience:** Designers, PMs, founders interested in AI/data product design

### 4. Targeting: Broad, Not Niched
- **Keywords:** "AI product designer," "data-intensive UX," "fintech designer," etc.
- **Advantage:** Larger addressable market
- **Tradeoff:** More competition (offset by authority-building via content)

### 5. Quick Wins vs. Compound Growth
- **Year 1 Goals:**
  - 12 Substack articles published
  - 500+ newsletter subscribers
  - Rank for 3-5 primary keywords
  - 500+ monthly organic traffic from search
  - 2-3 guest posts on authority sites
  - Case studies optimized + serving as SEO assets

---

## What's NOT in This Strategy

❌ **On-site blog** — Substack replaces this (better distribution + owned audience)  
❌ **Quick 30-day wins** — This is 12-month, compound growth strategy  
❌ **Paid advertising** — Organic focus (lower cost, higher quality leads)  
❌ **Social media content** — Not primary channel (LinkedIn / Twitter supportive only)
