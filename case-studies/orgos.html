Operating System for Transparent Organizations

Product Strategy · Enterprise SaaS · Organizational Design

Product Strategy · Enterprise SaaS · Organizational Design · 2026

I led product design for OrgOS, an operating system for radically transparent organisations — open finances, peer-set salaries, decentralised decisions, zero managers. Partnering with four engineers and one PM, I designed the information architecture for eight modules that let a 120-person team self-organise. The argument was the product: hierarchy isn't the only coordination mechanism; transparency can be. Client is under NDA. Happy to walk you through the framework, the eight modules, and the tradeoffs we made.
